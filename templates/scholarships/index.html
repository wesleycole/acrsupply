Anna Bullock Memorial Scholarship

The Anna Bullock memorial scholarship was established by ACR Supply Company to honor the life of Anna Bullock, remembered as a “standout student, athlete and daughter of Wayne and Ruth Ann Bullock.” This program is for the sons and daughters of ACR Supply Company associates. The program awards a $2000.00 scholarship annually to each student who is pursuing a formal two or four year degree.
