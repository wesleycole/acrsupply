COVID-19 Information

Due to our growing concerns over COVID-19 (the coronavirus) ACR Supply is taking proactive steps to protect the health of you, our teams, and all who visit our locations. ACR is deemed an Essential Business and we are under a Stay-at-Home order. We are committed to the HVAC/R industry and taking measures to remain open for business at all ACR locations. We have temporary guidelines in place during the current crisis:

ACR locations:

  • ACR Store locations are open with rotating staff from 7:00am-4:30pm.
  • Emergency service is available 24 hours: after hour’s number 1-800-442-4044.
  • Each location is conducting business using CDC recommendations for social distancing, sanitation, general hygiene and over all health of our teams and customers.
  • When possible, please use our ACR Estore for online orders. Visit www.acrsupply.com/request-account to sign up for an Estore account.
  • Call ahead or Estore orders will be picked and located in a general area for pick up.
  • Request a curbside pickup, we will bring the order to your vehicle.
  • We still have delivery options, call ahead for details.
  • Showrooms are open at this time and we are continuing to monitor this to ensure the safety of our team and customers.

Territory managers, Inside Sales and Product Managers

  • We will continue to conduct business using CDC recommendations on customer sites while utilizing webinar tools for customers meetings. TM’s are available to take phone orders or Estore orders.
  • Inside Sales teams are still taking orders, emails and phone calls to help you facilitate your needs.
  • Product managers will continue conducting business using CDC recommendations on job sites while utilizing webinar tools when needed.

Training:

  • All April training has been postponed and will be rescheduled soon.
  • Visit www.acrsupply.com/acr-university for online training opportunities.

Due to the current crisis, ACR reserves the right to make changes without notice.

Please learn more about COVID-19 from resources at Hardi.
