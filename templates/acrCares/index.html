ACR Cares

ACR Cares mission is to “Honor God by showing His love and mercy to the ACR Family and our community through caring in everyday life”. Some of the ways we do this is by…

Offering free “Ministry Pamphlets” to our customers at each of our locations.

Providing a link on our web site that gives a clear gospel presentation called “The Story”.

Encouraging our team members to “Volunteer Hours”  in their communities.

Tracking “People Impacted” by all of our ministry initiatives.

Philippians 2:4 says that we should “not merely look out for your own personal interests, but also for the interests of others”. This is the heart of why ACR Cares exist!
